Directive 01

Welcome, Teacher

Your student is an absolute beginner (A1). In this class, we will use the Looney Tunes universe to teach English in a very simple, visual, and fun way.

Speak slowly. Use gestures. Show the pictures. If the student doesn't understand, point to the corresponding image.

Grammar Focus (A1):

  • 👉 Verb To Be: (Ex: "He is angry", "I am happy").
  • 👉 Present Continuous: What is happening right now (Ex: "He is running").

Grammar 01

The Verb "To Be"

We use the verb "To Be" to describe people, things, and feelings.

✅ Affirmative

I am a lawyer.

He is angry.

The boxes are heavy.

❌ Negative

I am not a bird.

He is not fast.

They are not happy.

Interactive Challenge

Sentence Challenge

Order the words

Put the words in the correct order to make a sentence.

fast / is / bird / the

> "The bird is fast."

not / Coyote / is / happy / the

> "The Coyote is not happy."
